French Toast Recipe

Description

French toast is an incredibly easy and tasty recipe to make that can be upgraded with many different things. French toast only needs 6 quality ingredients and a skillet. Most of these ingredients are on hand already.

French toast can be made with any variety of bread and should be served hot with butter amd maple syrup

Ingredients

  • White bread
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Vanilla and Cinnamon
  • Salt
  • Butter

Directions

  1. Whisk milk, eggs, vanilla, cinnamon, and salt together in a shallow bowl.
  2. Lightly butter a griddle and heat over medium-high heat.
  3. Dunk bread in the egg mixture, soaking both sides. Transfer to the hot skillet and cook until golden, 3 to 4 minutes per side. Serve hot.
